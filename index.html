Witnessing Over a Century of History

Convocation Hall not only witnessed the graduation of tens of thousands of students over the subsequent deccades, it has also been the vital centre for a multitude of campus activities of local, national and international significance. Convocation Hall has come to represent the whole of the University; the building to which all alumni can relate. The focal point of our collective heritage, it continues to make a unique contribution to this great University.
