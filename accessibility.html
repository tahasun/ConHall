Accessible Entrance

Dark wood door between collonade and Simcoe hall entrance, Faces north towards the field and has designated parking directly infront of it

  • entrance at grade, with more powerful doors inside
  • accessible washroom to right inside this entrance
  • leads to main rotunda (turn left once inside)

Main Entrance

There are 3 more wood doors on the SE side, facing King's College Road, generally used as exit doors

  • 3 wood doors under the column facing the field
  • 2 steps up from the road (King's College Circle) leads to the interior rotunda
  • heavy manual doors which are not powered
