Getting Around

Accessible Entrance

Dark wood door between collonade and Simcoe hall entrance, Faces north towards the field and has designated parking directly infront of it

  • Entrance at grade, with more powerf doors inside
  • Accessible washroom to right inside this entrance
  • Leads to main rotunda (turn left once inside)
  • Leads to accessible seating on floor level of the main hall
  • There is no interior elevator in the hall and no accessible seating in the main hall tiers or in the balconies

Main Entrance

3 wood doors under the column facing the field. There are 3 more wood doors on the SE side, facing King's College Road, generally used as exit doors

  • 2 steps up from the road (King's College Circle)
  • Leads to the interior rotunda
  • Heavy manual doors which are not powered

Universal Washroom

wc accessible
  • There are two single stall universal washrooms with power doors
  • The accessible washroom is just inside the accessible entrance to the right
  • The second accessible washroom is in the rotunda hallway
